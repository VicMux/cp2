And yeah

Date Wed 08 February 2017

And yeah, I yearn to write about fragrant flowers in the spring, about the winter wonder landscape, of lazy summers and the bluest skies. I do, I do, I swear, I do. What spews forth instead is a diatribe, against life, death and everything in between.

Visualize and then describe in words the beauty of a lonely autumn afternoon on the bench in the park will be a breeze to most of you talented folk. Leaves falling gently on the ground as though taking care not to bruise the ground, lovers walking, arms around each other, pausing to pick up a leaf and marvel at its perfection, a large dog ignoring its owners entreaties to fetch the ball, coming and laying its head on my feet, a riot of images run through my mind. All those words seem hollow and untrue. I saw no beauty in any of that, what I take for granted is Mother nature, human nature and existing goodwill among all.

I am not making myself out to be a dark and mysterious person, turned on only by misery and misfortune, in fact I am a very dreamy soul with a streak of romanticism I try very hard to hide. I make friends easily and seek out company. My malady begins and ends with my writing.
