My Two Selves

Date Fri 10 February 2017

My Two Selves

We are, we were, the same, one self,
Seeking different destinies
Yearning intangibles, life, joys
Life intense, my heart tumbling along
Stumbling, tangling in wires
Barbed and meshed and bloodied
While the cruel calculating mind
Licking each fresh wound tasting blood, liking
And counting every false step and fall
Marches ahead and behind, same time
Destinies, parallel, will never meet or cross
The whole package, mind, heart, body ravaged
Lives, breathes, exists
In void.

One and the Same

My stories told a tale, you
Wanted to tell too
Of hearts, broken
Dreams shaken
Real and unreal ,fears
Shed and unshed tears
Your thoughts guide my lines
My pains show in your rhymes
We share a mind you and I Bound together in a secret tie
My passion, my muse
My lies, my ruse
For better and for worse
In letter and in prose
Wedded we are
In verse.

Days Bygone

Smells, familiar, follow
No hiding place safe from
Nostalgia, attacking senses
Hot palms clamp ears shut
Eyes tightly closed
Longest strides covering
Pasts, years, moments
Racing, erasing times
To be beaten again
And again
By that one moment
That one scent, soundless
Prevailing, assailing
No escaping joyous
Memories.
